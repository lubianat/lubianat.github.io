Hello, I'm Tiago Lubiana and this is my web page, welcome! 

I'm currently a PhD candidate at the University of São Paulo, working with Wikidata and the ontological modelling of concepts related to cell types. One way of know more about what I have been doing is via the [Scholia profile](https://scholia.toolforge.org/author/Q90076935) about me, auto generated from Wikidata.

I was also one of the creators of the [WikiProject COVID-19 on Wikidata](https://www.wikidata.org/wiki/Wikidata:WikiProject_COVID-19), for which you can most definitely contribute :). 

If you liked (or disliked) any of the content here, cool, I wanna know about it! Reach me via your social media of preference via the links on the left.

For the page of the lab where I'm currently based, go to the [Computational Systems Biology Laboratory Website](https://csbiology.com).

This site is under construction, so you might find a few bugs here and there. I apologize in advance.
