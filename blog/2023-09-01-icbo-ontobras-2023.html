ICBO and Ontobras 2023

Source: LinkedIn
Date: 2023-09-01

Last week I had the great opportunity to join two conferences at the same time: ICBO and Ontobras!

I worked on two different sessions:

  • The OBO Academy tutorial, alongside Nicolas Matentzoglu, Nicole Vasilevsky, and others.
  • The CELLS workshop on cells in the experimental life sciences, with Alexander Diehl, David Osumi-Sutherland, and others.
Both sessions brought up fruitful discussions and lots of learning.

I also had the opportunity to present a poster on connecting Wikidata and the Cell Ontology and spread the word about Wikidata alongside Erika Guetti (pictured below with the Wiki Movimento Brasil flag).

It was outstanding to connect with the Brazilian ontology community, including César Bernabé, Claudenir Fonseca, Prof. Giancarlo Guizzardi, and others. It made me proud of my origins in the state of Espírito Santo! Amazing what the NEMO research group has constructed over the years.

Finally, I had inspiring conversations with researchers whose work I've read so much, especially Barry Smith (virtually) and Stefan Schulz. I’m sure that from now on, I’ll read their articles with a new perspective in mind.

A big thanks to Fernanda Farinelli, Amanda Damasceno de Souza, and all others involved in the organization!
