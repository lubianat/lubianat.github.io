It was a fun, straightforward project of transforming cell type markers on PanglaoDB into semantic knowledge on Wikidata. Once the information is there, we could run some SPARQL queries to navigate the connected pieces. In the paper, we detailed the integration process and highlighted a few possible SPARQL queries and their results.
