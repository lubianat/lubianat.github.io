ISB Newsletter Highlights - April 2024

Source: ISB Newsletter
Date: 2024-04-15

The International Society for Biocuration newsletter is out! Here are some highlights:

  • Next biocuration conference scheduled at the Stowers Institute for Medical Research on 5th - 9th April, 2025 (save the date!)
  • Nicolas Matentzoglu Microgrant for the ICBO Ontology Tutorial
  • Mary Ann Tuli's inclusivity grant for attending Biocuration India
  • Blog posts on Biocuration India by Thea Fennell, Christopher Hunter, and the omnipresent Charles Tapley Hoyt
  • The Exceptional Contribution to Biocuration Award to Pascale Gaudet and Sandra Orchard
  • Reports by travel grantees of Biocuration India 2024 (including mine)

It's always good to see the community thriving. So glad to be a part of it!
