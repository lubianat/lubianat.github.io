PhD Defense and Next Steps

Source: LinkedIn
Date: 2024-09-09

For the first time in 27 years, I am not a student! This Monday I defended my PhD thesis on Wikidata and its role in bioinformatics, with a focus on cell types and the Human Cell Atlas, finalizing my formal education path.

I am very grateful for the opportunity, and I thank Helder Nakaya and FAPESP for the support and the committee members (Yesid Cuesta Astroz, Helena Brentani, and Jose Eduardo Santarem Segundo) for the evaluation and considerations. I thank especially my family, complete outsiders to the academic bubble, but that always believed in the power of education. Now let's see if I can get a job!

Jokes aside, it has been immensely fun, and the Universidade de São Paulo has become a true alma mater for me. I am eager to see the directions that life will take me :).

As a spoiler, it will likely involve some philosophical work with Prof. João Cortese, investigating the roots of our notions of "cell type", and how ontologies/Wikibase can be leveraged for that. I am waiting for a response from FAPESP on the scholarship (it has been +100 days already) to figure out when and how.
