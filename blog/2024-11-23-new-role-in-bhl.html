New Role as Wikimedian-in-Residence for the Biodiversity Heritage Library
Source: LinkedIn
Date: 2024-11-23

I am starting a new role as a Wikimedian-in-Residence for the Biodiversity Heritage Library, supported by the Smithsonian Libraries and Archives (SLA)!

For the next 6 months, I will assist the BHL-Wiki partnership in connecting their outstanding corpus to the Wikimedia Foundation ecosystem. The focus will be on the 300,000+ beautiful images of classic biodiversity works and improving their metadata quality via a data model including Structured Data on Wikimedia Commons and linked data on Wikidata.

I am very excited about this role. It is a dream job, combining the data skills I acquired during my PhD with a lifelong passion for biodiversity! If you are curious about the work I am doing, about BHL, and/or Wikipedia, please reach out!
