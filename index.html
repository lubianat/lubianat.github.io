Tiago Lubiana

I am currently a Wikimedian-in-Residence at the Biodiversity Heritage Library. I have recently received my PhD at the University of São Paulo, working with Wikidata and the ontological modeling of concepts related to cell types. I have lots of fun coding, building software, and wrangling structured knowledge. I find the life sciences to be exquisitely beautiful and believe in open knowledge as a tool for happier lives.
